🎵 Music Discovery Service

Startup: Fetches 30 tracks
Hourly: Fetches 10 tracks
Library Cap: 100 unused tracks max
Usage: One-time per video, deleted after use

🔄 Sweep & Recovery

Every 5 min: Reset stuck "processing" >15min → pending
Every 30 min: Retry queued/partial → processed
Manual: POST /sweep triggers immediate cycle
Max retries: 3 per submission
