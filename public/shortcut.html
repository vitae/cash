Submit Reel

Share flow arts videos directly from your iPhone camera roll to our YouTube Shorts channel. One tap. No browser needed.

Create the Shortcut

1

Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone. Tap + to create a new shortcut. Name it "Submit Reel"

2

Tap the info (i) button at the top. Turn on "Show in Share Sheet". Under "Types", select only Videos

3

Add action: Ask for Input
Type: Text
Prompt: "@instagram handle"

4

Add action: URL https://cash-production-680c.up.railway.app/quick-upload?handle= Then tap the URL and add the "Provided Input" variable (from step 3) at the end

5

Add action: Get Contents of URL
URL: URL from step 4
Method: POST
Request Body: File
File: Shortcut Input (the video from share sheet)

6

Add action: Show Notification
Title: "Reel Submitted!"

How to Use

1

Open Photos, select your flow video(s)

2

Tap Share → scroll down → tap "Submit Reel"

3

Type your @instagram handle

4

Your reel uploads automatically, gets EDM music added, and posts to YouTube Shorts
